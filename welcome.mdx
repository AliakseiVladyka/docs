## Welcome to Sabina

Sabina is your AI recruiter, designed to help teams run scalable, efficient interviews. From first setup through advanced automation, these docs walk you through how to configure Sabina, create interviews, and integrate with your existing tools.

Use this page as a launchpad to:

- Learn the core features of Sabina
- Follow step-by-step tutorials to build your first AI interview
- Explore best practices to improve candidate experience and decision quality
- Integrate Sabina with your ATS and other systems via the API

## Feature areas

Sabina’s docs are organized into several key areas:

- **Tutorials** – Guided walkthroughs for creating interviews, setting up workflows, and managing configuration.
- **Features overview** – Deep dives on capabilities like cheat detection, resume fit, and the Ask Sabina assistant.
- **Technical setup** – Guides for domains, SAML SSO, and other organization-level settings.
- **Automations & integrations** – How to connect Sabina to your ATS and automate job creation and candidate invitations.
- **API reference** – Endpoints, authentication, and webhooks for programmatic access.
