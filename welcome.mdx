## Welcome to Alex

Alex is your AI recruiter, designed to help teams run scalable, efficient interviews. From first setup through advanced automation, these docs walk you through how to configure Alex, create interviews, and integrate with your existing tools.

Use this page as a launchpad to:

- Learn the core features of Alex
- Follow step-by-step tutorials to build your first AI interview
- Explore best practices to improve candidate experience and decision quality
- Integrate Alex with your ATS and other systems via the API

## Feature areas

Alex’s docs are organized into several key areas:

- **Tutorials** – Guided walkthroughs for creating interviews, setting up workflows, and managing configuration.
- **Features overview** – Deep dives on capabilities like cheat detection, resume fit, and the Ask Alex assistant.
- **Technical setup** – Guides for domains, SAML SSO, and other organization-level settings.
- **Automations & integrations** – How to connect Alex to your ATS and automate job creation and candidate invitations.
- **API reference** – Endpoints, authentication, and webhooks for programmatic access.
